# survey-form

This survey form is designed to collect opinions and feedback from users in a clear and simple way. It starts with basic details, like name, email, and number, so that you can easily identify respondents or follow up with them. Next, users can select their favorite boy group, with options provided in an easy dropdown.
The form also invites participants to share their emotions, from being happy to feeling neutral, so you can get a sense of how they’re currently feeling. There’s a section where users can check off their interests—things like music, sports, art, or technology—and they can even type in their own interests if something isn’t listed.
Users are asked to rate their love for music on a sliding scale from 1 to 10, making it a bit interactive. There’s also a spot to provide open-ended feedback about how the survey itself could be improved, along with a dropdown for how satisfied they are with the survey.
